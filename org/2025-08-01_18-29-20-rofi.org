Rofi is a window switcher, run dialog, ssh-launcher and [[id:5b98a2b0-c3af-409d-ad95-1d1f0c3614da][dmenu]] replacement. Rofi is mainly used on [[id:3ce7a6a5-ae8b-4e02-9959-9b3e8d9705ac][Xorg]], but a patch has been made available so it can also be used in [[id:3492ec38-6a97-4bd1-8c9c-65c091e0aeb1][wayland]].
