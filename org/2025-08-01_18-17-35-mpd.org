MPD (music player daemon) is an audio player that has a server-client architecture. It plays [[id:0e4b5c15-bdde-4939-ab24-6fd35f085281][audio]] files, organizes playlists and maintains a music database, all while using very few resources. In order to interface with it, a separate client is needed.
