GNU Guix is a package manager for [[id:65db4594-b7ec-463b-9e97-64d080e44c12][GNU/Linux]] systems. It is designed to give users more control over their general-purpose and specialized computing environments, and make these easier to reproduce over time and deploy to one or many devices.
